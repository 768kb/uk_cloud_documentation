    UKCloud for Red Hat OpenShift

    Efficiently refactor, rearchitect or build new portable cloud native applications

    As a developer-focused Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) offering, UKCloud for Red Hat OpenShift helps to increase the agility and efficiency of your software development lifecycle – giving you the tools you need to adopt DevOps best practice. By divorcing development and application delivery from infrastructure, you can start to move your IT teams up the technology stack and start to focus on longer-term business value and strategic projects.

    Combining our secure and sovereign cloud with Red Hat's award-winning open source container platform, you can efficiently progress your modernisation journey from software to services, through to delivering innovative digital services and cloud-native applications.

    Additional options

    To get the most out of our platform, see below for some additional options and products you might want to consider using with UKCloud for Red Hat OpenShift (additional charges apply).

    Product options

    Complementary products

    Additional Control Plane Nodes. An operational cluster requires a minimum of three Control Plane Nodes. Depending on your workloads or solution, you may need more.

    Additional Infrastructure Nodes. Infrastructure Nodes are not mandatory, but we recommend a minimum of two to provide integrated cluster services. You may want more if you require additional services.

    Additional Application Nodes. You need at least one Application Node to run your application, however we recommend a minimum of two nodes for resilience. You may need more for duplicated or new applications.

    Registry storage. The OpenShift image registry persistent storage is hosted on UKCloud Cloud Storage.

    Tier 1 storage. Improved performance for workloads requiring higher disk throughput and IOPS.

    Managed IT Operations. Reduce the amount of time spent on managing your IT infrastructure.

    Security Operations Service. People, processes and tooling to assist with cyber monitoring capabilities.

    Professional Services. Help with solution design and digitisation projects.

    Private Cloud. For workloads that require custom configurations, such as large instances, or complex licensing requirements.

    Cloud Storage. Cost effective unstructured object storage for data archive.
