    UKCloud for VMware

    Supporting data centre modernisation with known and familiar technology

    The first step in data centre modernisation is rehosting your traditional applications. By removing the burden of having to maintain your own data centre, you and your team can start to focus on more strategic projects. And for applications where VMware is a critical component, UKCloud offers a secure, government-grade platform for you to transition to the cloud.

    With UKCloud for VMware you can take the first steps on your data centre modernisation and digital transformation journeys. With the right digital foundations, you can harness the latest innovative technologies to deliver improved outcomes with the added benefit and assurance of it running on a secure and sovereign cloud.
